Our Technology Stack

Advanced systems that power accurate diagnostics

Barcode Sample Tracking

Every sample receives a unique barcode at collection. This digital identity follows the sample through every stage—accessioning, processing, analysis, and reporting—eliminating the risk of sample mix-ups and ensuring complete traceability.

  • Unique identification for each sample
  • Real-time tracking through all stages
  • Zero sample mix-up guarantee

Calibrated Analyzers

Our diagnostic equipment is calibrated regularly according to manufacturer specifications and international guidelines. This ensures that every measurement is precise and reproducible, giving you results you can trust.

  • Regular calibration schedules
  • Manufacturer-certified protocols
  • Documented maintenance records

Internal Quality Control (IQC)

Daily quality control runs with certified control materials ensure our analyzers perform within acceptable limits. Westgard rules are applied to detect systematic errors before they can affect patient results.

  • Daily QC runs with certified materials
  • Westgard multi-rule monitoring
  • Immediate corrective actions

External Proficiency Testing (EQAS)

We participate in external quality assessment schemes that compare our results with laboratories worldwide. This independent verification ensures our performance meets global benchmarks.

  • Regular EQAS participation
  • Comparison with global peers
  • Continuous improvement

Biohazard Safety Protocols

Patient and staff safety is non-negotiable. Our laboratory follows strict biosafety protocols that protect everyone from potential exposure to infectious materials.

Personal Protective Equipment

All staff wear appropriate PPE including gloves, lab coats, and face shields when handling samples.

Safe Waste Disposal

Biohazardous waste is segregated, treated, and disposed of according to regulatory guidelines.

Decontamination Protocols

Regular decontamination of work surfaces, equipment, and spill management procedures.
