About Us

Two decades of trusted diagnostics, serving the Konch community with care

Our Story

Health Care Diagnostic Center was founded over two decades ago with a simple yet powerful mission: to bring quality diagnostic services to the Konch community in Gaya, Bihar.

We recognized the need for quality healthcare services in our region, forcing patients to travel to Gaya or Patna for basic tests. Our goal was to bridge this gap by bringing modern technology and trained professionals right here to Konch.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped who we are today

2005

Foundation

Health Care Diagnostic Center was established in Konch with a vision to provide quality diagnostics to the local community.

2013

Partner Lab Network

Became certified collection partners for major national diagnostic chains, expanding test accessibility for our community.

2018

Technology Upgrade

Introduced fully automated analyzers and implemented barcode-based sample tracking system for enhanced accuracy.

2021

Digital Transformation

Launched digital reports, online booking, and WhatsApp integration for seamless patient experience.

Today

Continuing the Legacy

Serving the Konch community with 5 partner labs, 500+ tests, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Our Impact on Konch Community

For over 20 years, we've been more than just a diagnostic center—we've been a trusted healthcare partner for thousands of families in Konch and surrounding areas.

We understand the unique healthcare challenges of our community and have tailored our services to meet those needs. From affordable pricing to convenient home collection services, we strive to make quality diagnostics accessible to everyone.
